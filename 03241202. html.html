Welcome to ICST

The Institute of Computer Science and Technology (ICST) is a premier institution dedicated to excellence in computer science education in Sri Lanka.

Established in 2000, ICST has been at the forefront of technology education, producing highly skilled professionals who contribute to the nation's digital transformation.
